Short Description:
Night mode for PDF files. Apply Dark, Sepia, or Warm filters for comfortable reading without hurting your eyes.

Long Description:
Tired of burning your eyes reading PDFs at night?

NightInk is a simple Chrome extension that adds night mode themes to any PDF file. Whether you're reading textbooks, research papers, or boring manuals — NightInk gives you control.

✨ Features:
• Dark, Sepia, and Warm filters
• Adjustable brightness and theme strength
• Automatically runs only on PDF pages
• Clean interface — no tracking, no ads, no fluff

Get NightInk and read comfortably — day or night.
